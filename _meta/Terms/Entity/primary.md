An entity is **something that exists as itself**. 

# In computer science
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Entity#In_computer_science
In computer science, an entity is an **object that has an identity**, **which is independent of the changes of its attributes**. 
It represents **long-lived information** relevant for the users and is usually stored in a database.
